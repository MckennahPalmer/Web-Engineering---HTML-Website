There are four stylesheets that you may select from. Each one will change the coloring, fonts, and even the layout of the page.You can change the layout of this page by selecting different stylesheets in the menu, or by selecting one of the following links:
Stylesheet 1, Stylesheet 2, Stylesheet 3, Stylesheet 4.

Instructions

All the stylesheets have a different background color, text size, font family, border-style, amount of padding, and blockquote parameters. The first stylesheet is a grey background, grooved border, fantasy font, 10px padding, and the smallest dimemsions for a blockquote. The second stylesheet is a yellowish background, solid border, Times New Roman font, 20px padding, and slightly larger dimemsions for the blockquote. The third stylesheet is a green background, ridge border, sans serif font, 30px padding, and slightly larger dimemsions for the blockquote. The fourth stylesheet is beige background, double border, cursive font 30px padding, and the largest dimemsions for the blockquote.
