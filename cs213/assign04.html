Math Wizards Tutoring

Who we are

A group of students who love math so much that they want to make teaching math and helping other students an part time job.

What we do

We take appointments from the form on our website, and then call to verify that the one on one or group study will happen. We will go over any homework, questions or concepts with you, and you will understand it before the end of the meeting.

Why we care

While we do find math a lot of fun, it is even more so to helping a student and then seeing the dawn of understanding cross there face. Our goal is to help any student who has a question to understand it the best way possible, and to be able to not dread doing math.
